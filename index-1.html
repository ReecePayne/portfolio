Hey! I am Reece Payne. I study Games Design and I have a passion to create games which are fun for everyone.

Video games & gaming in general have been one of my biggest passions for as long as I can remember. I have been interested in the behind the scenes of game development ever since I started trying to play games more seriously and that made me think of how gameplay mechanics could be designed differently and what would be more effective making my passion as a want to be gameplay programmer even stronger. I enjoy working in teams and I can thrive in environments where we can all learn together even if its a miniscule amount of experience.

I have been using Unity & C# to develop personal & college projects for almost a year now, prior to Unity development, I had been using Unreal Engine 4 for a year whilst doing my 1st year of my Games Design course, using both visual scripting and C++. I prefer C# & C++ over visual scripting since it feels more physical and that you are actually creating something yourself from the ground up no matter how small it is and that gives me the thrill and drive to develop more and more.
